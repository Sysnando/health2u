01 · The engine

Any document. Any language. One structure.

Snap a photo, forward an email, drop a PDF. Our medical-grade AI reads the document, identifies every biomarker, converts units, translates terminology, and stores it in an open, standardized schema — the same way, every time.

“Colesterol LDL · 118 mg/dL” from a Brazilian lab and “LDL-C · 3.05 mmol/L” from a French one become the same data point on the same chart.

02 · How it works

Three steps. That's the whole thing.

STEP 01
Send it in.
Upload a PDF, photograph a paper report, or forward the lab's email. We accept everything — paper, fax printouts, DICOM, handwritten notes.
STEP 02
We read it.
Our AI interprets the document in its original language, extracts every biomarker, and maps it to the international LOINC / SNOMED standard. Units are normalized automatically.
STEP 03
You understand it.
Everything lands in one clean timeline with trends, plain-language explanations, and proactive flags when values drift — years of records, readable in a glance.
